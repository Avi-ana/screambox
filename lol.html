ScreamBox Stream Analysis

Our stream, featuring horror Roblox games with fun punishments, lasted 2 hours. Team members: Kwynzy Gwyneth Avila, Ian Gabriel Ferranco, Yojiro Kyle Lubis, Iyesha Therese Dabu, Gred Azryj Bunales, Lukas Maiko Balmeo. This analysis evaluates performance and suggests improvements.

Key Strengths/Highlights

The stream was a success, with strong viewer interaction and engagement.

  • Viewers: 384
  • Comments: 73
  • Interactions: 190 (likes, shares, reactions)
  • Overall Rating: 4.78/5
  • Stream Vibe: 61.1% perceived it as funny with light frights.
  • Popular Highlights: Punishment segments and jumpscare reactions (33.3%).
  • Entertainment: 100% of respondents found it entertaining.
  • Most Enjoyed: Gameplay was the most enjoyed part.

Areas for Improvement

Several areas need improvement to enhance future streams.

  • Game Selection: Viewers want to vote on games.
  • Technical Issues: Audio imbalances, lags, and buffering were reported.
  • Stream Length: Viewers desired longer streams.
  • Visual Enhancements: Improve horror-themed presentation.
  • Punishment Randomness: Make punishments more unpredictable.

Engagement Analysis

Engagement was highest during jumpscares, punishment segments, and live Q&A.

  • Most Enjoyed Segments: Games (77.8%) and punishment sections (55.6%).
  • Engagement Rating: Majority rated engagement 4 or 5.
  • Key Engagement Elements: Games were the main driver.

Viewership Analysis

Technical glitches and content interaction impacted viewership.

  • Factors: Technical issues, viewer interaction, game selection.
  • Community Feedback: Positive community feeling, desire for viewer game voting.

Actionable Recommendations

  • Implement Viewer Voting: Allow viewers to choose games.
  • Upgrade Technical Setup: Improve audio and internet connection.
  • Extend Stream Length: Consider longer streams.
  • Enhance Visuals: Improve horror-themed presentation.
  • Randomize Punishments: Make punishments more unpredictable.
  • Continue Community Interaction: Keep engaging with viewers.
